\begin{abstract}
We introduce \SYS{}, a middleware framework for secure stream processing.
Its design builds on Intel's \emph{Secure Guard Extensions} (SGX) to guarantee the privacy and the integrity of the data being processed.
Our initial experimental results of \SYS{} are promising: the framework is easy to use, and delivers high throughput, enabling developers to implement complex processing pipelines in a few lines of scripting code.
\end{abstract}
